About AquaSensor
Delivering Real-Time Water Quality Data

AquaSensor, founded in 2023 and headquartered in Barnsley, UK, specializes in building end-to-end systems that provide accurate water quality data for rivers, lakes, and marine environments. Our mission is to deliver low-cost, real-time river quality monitoring solutions that ensure legal compliance, save money, and protect the environment.

Extensive monitoring of our river systems is essential both to provide a baseline of water quality and to give access to live data with alerts related to unusual changes. AquaSensor's solutions are designed to be accurate, live, long-term remote capable, and economically viable for all users of the system.

Our comprehensive approach includes sensor deployment, data analytics, and user-friendly interfaces, enabling stakeholders to make informed decisions to safeguard aquatic ecosystems. By leveraging advanced technology and a commitment to environmental stewardship, AquaSensor aims to be at the forefront of water quality monitoring.
